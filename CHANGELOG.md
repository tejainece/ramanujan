## 1.0.0

- **Boolean Path Operations**: Full polygon-clipping pipeline supporting Union, Intersection, Difference, Xor, Division, Fracture, and Flatten.
- **Offsetting & Stroke Expansion**: Inset/outset offsetting with round/miter/bevel corner joins, and stroke outline expansion with tapering and cap styles.
- **Path Smoothing & Utilities**: Catmull-Rom and Cardinal spline smoothing, corner rounding, and notch carving.
- **Curve Fitting**: Recursive fitting of arbitrary 2D point sequences to lines, circular arcs, and Béziers.
- **Parametric Segments**: Analytical solvers for line, circular arc, elliptical arc, quadratic Bézier, and cubic Bézier segment properties, intersection, and coincidence.
- **Geometry & Primitives**: 2D points, rectangles, circle/ellipse shapes, strong angle types, and Affine 2D transformations.
